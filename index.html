Welcome!

Hello, I’m Abdollah Eydi! I'm a passionate mechatronics engineer Interested in robotics, machine learning, deep learning, control theory and path planing. I received my M.Sc. from the Sharif University of Technology. There, I was a member of the Social and Cognitive Robotics Lab, lead by Prof. Ali Meghdari.
I was involved in developing a social assistance robot called Arash; that's a robot to help children with cancer. I collaborated on this project to develop a ROS-based software framework for working with the Arash robot and developing robot electronic boards. Besides, as part of my master project, I developed a sound source localization system to improve the robot's interaction with humans.
I have also collaborated on the Rasa robot project as an electronic board designer.
My other experience is working in a startup about cloud service and developing a framework to build android and ios apps.

A Social Robot to Assist Children with Cancer in Hospitals

Arash is a mobile social robot that has been designed for educational and therapeutic interventions for children with cancer in a hospital environment. Many children are hospitalized for different diseases; among these, children with cancer experience special conditions and needs due to complex and painful surgeries. In particular, they suffer from physical pain caused by both cancer and its treatment process. Moreover, cancer treatment causes emotional distress, which can reduce the efficiency of the treatments. Using social robots to interact with children in a hospital environment could decrease these children’s distress, thereby improving the efficiency of their treatment.

My contribution to the Arash project is designing a sound source localization module that enables the robot to have effective interaction with children. Besides, I designed the electrical structure of the robot and some PCB boards. Also, I have collaborated on Arash software development, and I have developed a variety of different software packages utilizing the ROS ecosystem.
